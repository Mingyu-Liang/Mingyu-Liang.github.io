Mingyu Liang

Computer Systems Laboratory

Biography

I am a second year Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Cornell University, advised by Prof. Christina Delimitrou. My research interest lies in cloud computing and computer architecture. Currently I am working on the benchmark characterization and root cause analysis of large-scale microservices. Before coming to Cornell, I obtained my B.S. degree in Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), working with Prof. Chao Li.
