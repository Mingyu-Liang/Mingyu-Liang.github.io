Biography

Hi, this is Mingyu! I am a fourth year Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Cornell University, advised by Prof. Christina Delimitrou. My research interest lies in cloud computing, computer systems and computer architecture. More recently, I am focusing on root cause analysis of large-scale microservices, workload characterization and benchmarking in datacenters. Before joining Cornell, I obtained my B.S. degree in Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), advised by Prof. Chao Li.
