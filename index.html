Biography

I received my Ph.D. at Cornell University in 2024, advised by Prof. Christina Delimitrou. I am broadly interested in improving the performance and efficiency of cloud systems. I was a research intern at Google Brain in 2021 and at Meta in 2022, 2024.

Before joining Cornell, I obtained my B.S. degree at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Chao Li.

I am now a software engineer at Google.
